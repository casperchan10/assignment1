Home

Ultra Singapore is an outdoor electronic music festival that debut in 2015 in Singapore as part of Ultra Music Festival's
world wide expansion.


The first edition of Ultra Singapore took place at Ultra Park in 2016. The event was held near Marina Bay Sands Hotel. This music festival hosted three stages:

-Main Stage
-Resistance Stage
-Live Stage

Line Up

-Main Stage houses the main headliner DJs
-Resistance Stage offers techno/house genre DJs
-Live Stage mainly involves band music and live instruments
